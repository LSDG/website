We develop software!

That's right! As the name implies, we're a group of developers, in Lubbock TX, who get together once a month to share ideas, and technology. It's the first Thursday, of every month, @ 6:30pm here.

Check us out!

Technology

We are huge fans of all technology. Everything from .Net to Node.js to Erlang, there's someone in the group who knows and loves it. We also believe in open-source, and all of our projects are available on Github. Feel free to browse and fork away.

Christmas Lights

Every year, we put up a Christmas Lights show, using a Raspberry Pi arm computer, and a microcontroller. People can drive by, and watch the lights as they blink to music we broadcast over a small radio transmitter. The show is entirely free, and volunteer.

Community

The LSDG is all about building a community. We know there's great developers, living in Lubbock, writing some amazing things. LSDG is a way for us to find each other, connect, and collaborate. You don't have to be an amazing developer, either. We welcome everyone.
